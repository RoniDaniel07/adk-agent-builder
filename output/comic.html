My ADK Comic

### Character Manifest * **Momotaro:** A peach-shaped birthmark on his left shoulder, a red *hachimaki* (headband) with a single white peach emblem, carries his Kibi-dango in a small, patterned cloth pouch tied to his waist. * **Old Woman:** A simple sky-blue kimono, hair tied in a bun with a single wooden Kanzashi pin, a small mole beside her left eye. * **Old Man:** Wears a traditional cone-shaped straw hat (*sugegasa*), a dark brown work vest (*hanten*) over his robes, always has a wood-cutting axe either in hand or slung over his back. * **Spotted Dog:** A rope collar with a small bronze bell, one black-spotted ear and one white ear, a distinctive scar over its right eye. * **Monkey:** Bright red face and hindquarters, wears a small, tattered green vest, constantly clutching a stolen persimmon (even after joining Momotaro). * **Pheasant:** Iridescent green and copper plumage, exceptionally long twin tail feathers tipped in gold, a sharp, silver-colored beak. * **King of the Oni:** Two massive, twisting horns of jagged black rock, wears spiked iron armor plates over a tiger-skin loincloth, wields a giant, gnarled iron club (*kanabō*).

### The Story of Momotaro: The Comic Script **Panel 1** **NARRATION:** Long ago, in a quiet village nestled between mountains and rivers, lived an old man and an old woman. They were kind and good, but their hearts were heavy with a silent wish. **SCENE:** A humble, traditional Japanese house. The OLD WOMAN sits weaving, looking wistfully out the window. The OLD MAN sharpens his axe nearby, a sad look on his face.

**Panel 2** **NARRATION:** One morning, as she washed clothes in the river, fate sent a ripple across the water. **SCENE:** The OLD WOMAN is kneeling by a clear, flowing river. Her eyes are wide with astonishment as she spots something extraordinary floating towards her: a peach, impossibly large and glowing with a faint, warm light.

**Panel 3** **NARRATION:** It was the most magnificent peach she had ever seen. **SCENE:** The OLD WOMAN, using a long bamboo laundry pole, struggles with great effort to guide the giant peach to the riverbank. The peach is nearly as big as she is.

**Panel 4** **NARRATION:** That evening, as they prepared to cut their miraculous bounty… **SCENE:** Inside their home, the OLD MAN raises a large knife over the peach, which sits on their floor. The OLD WOMAN clasps her hands in anticipation. The peach begins to glow brighter.

**Panel 5** **SFX:** *CRACK!* **SCENE:** The peach splits perfectly in half on its own, bathing the room in a soft, golden light. The old couple stumbles back in shock, shielding their eyes.

**Panel 6** **NARRATION:** …a voice, clear as a temple bell, spoke from within. **SCENE:** A healthy baby boy sits smiling where the pit should be. He raises a tiny hand. **BABY MOMOTARO:** Do not be afraid. The Heavens have sent me to be your son. **OLD COUPLE:** (Whispering) A son…

**Panel 7** **NARRATION:** And so they named him Momotaro, the Peach Boy. He grew into a young man of unparalleled strength and kindness. But a shadow loomed over the land. **SCENE:** A montage panel. On the left, a young MOMOTARO effortlessly carries a huge stack of firewood for his father. On the right, a dark, jagged island fortress (Onigashima) is visible across a stormy sea, with monstrous, horned silhouettes of Oni raiding the mainland coast.
